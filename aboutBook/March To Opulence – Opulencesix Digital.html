March To Opulence

March To Opulence, our first book, is a special assortment of classics, biographies, histories, speeches, and more, for the makers of a bright tomorrow. It is a unique selection of delightful, inspiring, and illuminating writings of the world, specially compiled for young sculptors of tomorrow’s world.

It is available in eBook or Paperback forms at Amazon.com, Amazon India (Special Indian market edition), Apple iBooks, or others. Continue reading for more information.

Overview

What are some of the finest tales across different genres from various cultures of the world? What makes authors like Tolstoy and Twain, Valmiki and Wilde, so special? How did icons of science like Nikola Tesla, or artists like Leonardo da Vinci evolve to greatness? What is Jalaluddin Rumi’s contribution to Sufi Islamic thought? Whether free thinking masses are a nuisance to world order, or, they often bring about avalanches of progress. Could Dostoevsky, or upheavals in France and India throw light on this?

These pages address all this and much more, through memorable extracts from classic literature, biographies, essays, histories, and speeches. Along with providing amusement, the range of overarching topics and varying opinions presented, will also help readers build a well rounded personality across multiple parameters of personal development. Potentially a permanent member of your household bookshelf; for young ones, as well as their guardians.

The Opulencesix team has leveraged its experience in aggregation and summarization of digital content such as reader opinions and popularity charts, to select extracts most appropriate for the book’s target audience and intent, with minimal personal bias.

FAQ

March To Opulence, What is it about?

The book is a collection of fine writings of the past across different genres, colorful writing styles, and cultures. Many overarching subjects of relevance to all, such as Wisdom, Science, Arts, Economics, Social Behavior, Politics, or Philosophy, are covered through amusing stories, essays, or speeches.

Why, such a collection?

We have always believed that, along with sound knowledge of one’s primary area of work, it is necessary for a person to have a broader view of the world. It is about developing some key traits that are very useful irrespective of field of work. Secondly, exposure to great creations that have stood the test of time helps in the development of independent thought and an overall refinement of taste. All ingredients so important in a person’s (and therefore society’s) ‘March To (all-round) Opulence’! In our opinion, pretty unique.

What is so unique about it? Classic Collections, Anthologies are not uncommon.

True to an extent. But it has a few ingredients that make it special. 1. Variety of topics, covered in such a short compilation of 42 chapters. Most are among highly acclaimed writings of the world. 2. Illustrations, Footnotes and the odd modification to ensure that even complex passages can be comprehended by a common person. 3. Chapters are woven around an underlying theme that combines amusement, instruction, and inspiration to strive for all-round development. 4. Special stress on building Prudence, Fearlessness, Wealth, General Awareness, Scientific Approach with Social Sensitivity, and Thirst for Truth and Excellence. An important ‘Food for Thought’ section to explicitly attract attention to these aspects and stimulate critical thinking.

Whom is it targeted toward?

The primary target of this book is teens and youth, the sculptors of tomorrow’s world, whom all of us are looking onto with great hope. The book has 3 sections. Roughly, the first 20 percent is for children and very young teens, the next 30% for teens and youth, and the third 50% for older teens and above. The later half of the book will be interesting and useful for adults too, especially the parents/guardians of these young ones. We dream about this collection becoming a permanent member of the household bookshelf 

Around 650 pages seems a bit on the higher side.

The size need not intimidate anyone. It has over 40 chapters, all fairly independent, from one another. Readers can go through the contents, read the preamble of a chapter (it also mentions the complexity of the text) and read those in any order as per preference, at leisure. Some readers might have previously read a few chapters included in the book. We consciously did not break up the book into multiple parts since we wanted a single self-contained guidebook for the entire household.

How did you come up with the list of extracts covered in this book?

We first did research on the kinds of topics needed to be covered, and their weightages, considering our target audience. After that we analyzed various popularity lists and other related material such as user and expert opinions, to come up with the final chapter mix. We tried our best to achieve maximal coverage with high quality, but minimal content volume. Of course, the final decider was our team’s personal judgement.

Could you list down some interesting contents?

Among others, it stars: Jatakas, Lewis Carroll, Tesla, Folk and fairy tales from east and west, Leonardo da Vinci-Beethoven, Tolstoy-Turgenev-Twain, Voltaire-Valmiki, Woodrow Wilson-Wilde, Sherlock Holmes, Gandhi-Savarkar-MN. Roy, Rumi, Capital/Commun/Human and other isms.

The ‘Food for Thought’ section at the end of each chapter. These are like exercises we find in textbooks right?

Somewhat, but a better way to look at these sections is to consider them as extensions to the main text. These explicitly try to provoke thought, related to the subject covered in the main text. Some questions are hard, without clear answers, occasionally uncomfortable too. But it is time we started boldly facing those, started discussing those openly with friends and elders, and finding innovative ways out.

Anything else that needs mention?

Yes. This book is just an initial push, to be realistic. We have listed around 100 references (approx 2000 hours of reading), to some highly acclaimed books, toward the end of this compilation. We would like to encourage readers to read these as much as they can, according to liking and convenience. Above all, readers, chill, be at ease. Be assured of finding ample of amusement, inspiration and guidance from towering personalities of the past, embedded in these pages.
